NAGY PRESS AND BOOKSHOP is a registered private business entity that is into website development, graphic designing printing and supply of customized t-shirts, cloth laboratory coats, books, receipts, pens, key holders, wrist bands, posters, banners, invitation cards, brooches, ID cards and any printing related activity. It is established in Kumasi (Ashanti Region) with a branch at Nsawkaw (Bono Region). Financial institutions, universities, colleges, private schools, corporate bodies and students' associations are at the center of our operations. We have over the years been supplying the souvenirs of the students of Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology (KNUST), University of Cape Coast (UCC). University of Development Studies (UDS), University of Renewable and Natural Resources (UENR), Sunyani Technical University (STU), Koforidua Technical University (KTU). Kintampo College of Health (COHK), Bolgatanga Technical University (BTU), Kumasi Technical University (KSTU). ST. Louis College of Education, and other corporate associations or institutions including BACSOD Ltd, Flip In Ltd, Graduate Students Association of Ghana (GRASAG), National Union of Ghana Students (NUGS), Campus Christians Fellowship (CCF), Pax Romana, Young Professionals and Youth Coalition 'YPYC) and many more.

We have good track records as far as quality, customer satisfaction, trustworthiness and punctuality in business is concerned. We deliver quality items, affordable and on time no matter your location.
